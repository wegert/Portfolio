Ball der Technik 2016

Full advertising presence for "Ball der Technik 2016" including a City Light, a Flyer, etc.

The "Ball der Technik" is one of the biggest, if not the biggest ball in Carinthia. Without good advertising, the ball wouldn't be as big.
Posters, flyers for sponsors and everyone interested, a City Light and a really big poster had to be created in order to be printed and displayed all over Carinthia.

Trinkwassertag 2016

A submition for a video competition about "Trinkwasser" for the "Trinkwassertag 2016".

My project partner and I had the opportunity to submit a video into a competition for explaining water and where it comes from.
We used programs such as Adobe AfterEffects, Adobe Premiere, Cinema4D and Flow to create flowing water and different animations. A drone and a standard DSLR helped us get the right footage we wanted.
We scored second out of all 27 submission. You can see the outcome on YouTube: Trinkwassertag 2016.
